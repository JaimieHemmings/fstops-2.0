About

Hi, I'm Ben Hollington, the proud owner of FStops Photography. A photography studio based in the heart of Cornwall, Great Britain. I provide a variety of photography services and enjoy capturing all sorts of stories.

My goal is simple

I capture real moments that feel like you. I want you to feel comfortable and just be yourself infront of the camera, whther it's on your wedding day, during a family session, or while working on a creative project together.

Photography has been my passion for as long as I can remember. Growing up surrounded by Cornwall's rugged coastlines and stunning countryside, I’ve always been drawn to the way light and landscape work together, shaping how we see and experience the world.

For over 15 years, I’ve been behind the camera, honing my craft and developing a deep appreciation for capturing life’s authentic, unscripted moments.

Whether it’s documenting a special event, showcasing the character of a home or business, or simply capturing the natural beauty of a landscape, my aim is to tell stories through images that feel real and meaningful.

I focus on the details that often go unnoticed

and I love the challenge of making each photograph unique to the moment or space it’s portraying. Along with Cornwall, I’ve also spent time living in the French Alps, which gave me the chance to explore new, dramatic landscapes and deepen my love for capturing nature in all its forms.

Based on Bodmin Moor, I draw constant inspiration from the natural world around me. I’m always looking for new ways to tell stories through my photography, and I’m excited to see where my camera will take me next.
